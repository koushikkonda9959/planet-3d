Plastic: A curse to life
Plastic was invented in recent past but came into our life very rapidly and now Its impact on our life has become so huge. Plastic is made of petro chemicals and takes several thousand years to degrade. Most of the used plastic end up in bins and reach Land-filling or burnt up or thrown into waters that often cause lots of pollution and harm to marine life. Hence we suggest to Reduce, ReUse and Recycle.
Substitute for plastic
A means to reduce plastic is to use alternatives to plastic. We use bioplastics that are made from plant extracts and provides every purpose of plastic with biodegradability and extended strength and resistance.
What is 3D printing?
It is an additive manufacturing technology to build any complex object in a single stretch. A 3D printing can be compared to traditional handlooms used to weave clothes by alignment of threads(1 dimension ) to form a cloth of 2D. Similarly, In 3D printing, a filament is melted and extruded through nozzle To form layers and stacked together to form a perfect 3D object.
